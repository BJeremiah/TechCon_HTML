History

Since its inception in 2010, TechCon has grown from a small gathering of innovators into one of the world’s leading technology conferences. It has provided a platform for groundbreaking discussions, product launches, and networking opportunities that have shaped the tech industry.

Mission

The mission of TechCon is to inspire and empower the global tech community. By bringing together visionaries, developers, and entrepreneurs, TechCon strives to accelerate innovation and address the challenges of tomorrow.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted influential leaders who have transformed the world of technology:

  • Dr. Ada Lovelace – Celebrated computer scientist and AI researcher, known for pioneering advancements in machine learning.
  • Elon Musk – Visionary entrepreneur behind Tesla and SpaceX, who shared insights on sustainable technology and space exploration.
  • Grace Hopper – Renowned programmer and innovator, recognized for her work on early programming languages and computer systems.
